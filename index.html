About Me


In the realm of data science, I orchestrate the symphony of data, transforming chaos into harmony to reveal the melodies of insight that drive innovation and progress.

As a Software Engineer with over 2.5 years of experience in DevOps Pipelines, I bring a robust skill set honed through hands-on work in various facets of software development and deployment. My journey in the tech industry has been marked by a commitment to continuous learning and growth, culminating in a Master’s Degree in Data Science. This educational pursuit has equipped me with advanced proficiency in Python, SQL, ML, and Data Analysis, positioning me as a versatile professional capable of tackling complex data challenges with precision and creativity.This rigorous program provided me with a solid foundation in data science principles, algorithms, and methodologies, empowering me to leverage cutting-edge techniques to extract valuable insights from data.

In my professional journey, I have demonstrated proficiency in a diverse array of technologies and tools essential for modern software development and data analysis. From database management using MySQL to orchestrating containerized applications with Docker and Kubernetes, I have navigated the intricacies of DevOps practices with ease. With a solid academic foundation and hands-on experience in Pioneering technologies, I am poised to make meaningful contributions to projects and organization.
